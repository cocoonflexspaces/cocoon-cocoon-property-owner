Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to partner with Cocoon?

Nothing. There are zero fees or costs to onboard your space. We only make money when you do — our commission comes out of the booking revenue, so you're never out of pocket.

What if my space is unavailable on certain dates?

Easy — just tell us the dates you want blocked. We always work around your schedule. You can block a single day or an entire month whenever you need to.

I don't want parties or loud events. What exactly do you book?

You choose. We present each inquiry to you before accepting anything. Most Cocoon bookings are quiet, high-profile, low-footprint: brand activations, chef dinners, intimate wedding receptions, editorial shoots, and private gatherings of 10–40 people.

How do I get paid?

Payments are sent bi-weekly, after each booking is completed and closed. No chasing, no delays — it lands in your account automatically.

Do I need to be present or involved during bookings?

Never, unless you want to be. Our team handles all communication, coordination, and on-site management. All you need to do is approve or decline incoming inquiries.

What happens if something gets damaged?

We require a $2M general liability COI and umbrella insurance from every client. One of our team members is on-site for the entire booking. Less than 1% of our bookings have ever had an incident — and every one was covered by the client's insurance.
